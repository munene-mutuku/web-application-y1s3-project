Why Choose KeshoSave?

Everything you need to manage your money, all in one place.

  • Smart Budgeting — Set monthly budgets for food, transport, rent, and more.
  • Savings Goals — Save toward school fees, a new phone, or a holiday — at your own pace.
  • Expense Tracking — Log every shilling you spend and see exactly where your money goes.
  • M-Pesa Integration — Link your Safaricom number and auto-import your M-Pesa transactions.
  • Spending Insights — Get weekly reports that show your spending patterns and help you improve.
  • Referral Rewards — Invite friends and earn bonuses every time they join KeshoSave.

How It Works

Up and running in under 5 minutes:

  1. Create your free account at keshosave.co.ke
  2. Link your M-Pesa number in Settings
  3. Set your monthly budget per category
  4. Log your daily expenses or let M-Pesa do it automatically
  5. Review your weekly Spending Insights report every Sunday
