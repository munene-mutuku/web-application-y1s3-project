Frequently Asked Questions

Is KeshoSave really free?

Yes. All core features — budgeting, savings goals, expense tracking, and M-Pesa integration — are completely free. No credit card is required.

How do I link M-Pesa?

After signing up, go to Settings → Linked Accounts and enter your Safaricom number. You will receive a confirmation prompt on your phone to complete the link.

Is my data safe?

Yes. KeshoSave uses bank-level encryption to protect your data. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Can I use KeshoSave for my business?

Absolutely. Select Business as your account type when signing up to access features designed for freelancers and small businesses.
