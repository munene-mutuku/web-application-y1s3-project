How KeshoSave Works

Getting started with KeshoSave takes less than 5 minutes. Follow the steps below to set up your account, link M-Pesa, and start saving toward your goals today.

Your Step-by-Step Setup Guide

  1. Create Your Free Account

    Visit our Sign Up page and fill in your name, email address, and phone number. Choose between a Personal or Business account type. No credit card required — KeshoSave is completely free.

  2. Link Your M-Pesa Number

    After signing up, go to Settings → Linked Accounts and enter your Safaricom number. You will receive an M-Pesa PIN prompt to confirm. Once linked, your M-Pesa transactions are imported automatically every day.

  3. Set Up Your Monthly Budget

    Tap Budgets and create spending limits for each category: food, transport, rent, entertainment, and more. KeshoSave will alert you when you are approaching a limit.

  4. Create a Savings Goal

    Tap Goals and choose what you are saving for — school fees, a new phone, a holiday, or anything else. Set a target amount and a deadline. KeshoSave calculates how much to save each week to reach your goal on time.

  5. Log Daily Expenses

    Tap Add Expense to record any cash transactions that M-Pesa does not capture automatically. The more you log, the more accurate your Spending Insights will be.

  6. Review Your Weekly Insights

    Every Sunday you receive a Spending Insights report showing how much you spent, in which categories, and how your savings goals are progressing. Use this to adjust your budget for next week.

Pro Tips for New Users

  • Start with just one savings goal — keep it simple.
  • Enable M-Pesa auto-import so you don't have to log every transaction manually.
  • Check your Spending Insights every Sunday before planning next week's budget.
  • Use the Referral Rewards feature to earn bonuses when you invite friends.
